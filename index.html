FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Here's everything you need to know about the Toyota Music Fest!


How can I get tickets to the event?

All you have to do is register online to secure your ticket. The Toyota Music Fest is FREE!

What should I wear?

Rain or shine, make sure you look fine.

What's the full lineup of the Toyota Music Fest?

Catch your favorite artists perform LIVE!

  • The Ransom Collective
  • December Avenue
  • Ben&Ben
  • IV of Spades
  • Cueshé
  • Silent Sanctuary
  • Sponge Cola
  • Hale
  • Rivermaya
  • Ebe Dancel
  • Callalily
  • 6cyclemind
  • Parokya ni Edgar
  • Ely Buendia

Performances begin at 3:00 PM.


Will I be able to re-enter the event if I leave the venue?

Unfortunately, you will not be allowed to enter the event premises once you exit.

What items are prohibited during the event?

All attendees must submit themselves to security for inspection. The following items will not be allowed during the event:
  • Drink and food containers
  • Plastic or glass bottles
  • Alcoholic drinks and cigarettes
  • Vape
  • Lighters and matches
  • Bags must not exceed 8" x 8"
  • Action and professional cameras
  • Laser pens
  • Items that may cause injury to others
  • Sharp objects
  • Selfie sticks
  • Batons
  • Long umbrellas
  • Tear gas
  • Pepper spray
  • Tasers
  • Firearms & weapons
  • Illegal substances
  • Drugs without prescriptions

People who are unruly, under the influence of alcohol or prohibited drugs, and pose a threat to themselves and others during the event will also not be allowed to enter.

What do we do in case of an emergency?

We have prepared medical stations and ambulances around the venue to assist you in case of an emergency.

What should I bring?

Don’t forget these essentials during the Toyota Music Fest:
  • A printed copy of your e-ticket to secure your event passes.
  • 1 valid ID to verify your ticket.

Note: DSLR cameras and professional recording equipment are not allowed inside the venue. Bring yourself and your friends of course!

Is there an age limit to attendees?

The event is open to all those who are 13 years and above! Please note minors 13-17 years old will need adult supervision to enter the event.

Can I bring my own food and drinks to the event?

To secure the safety of our guests only food and drinks from our food concessionaires are allowed inside the venue.

Will there be official photographers during the event?

Yes, there will be official photographers during the Toyota Music Fest to capture all the fun moments.

Note: Attendees agree that any photo or video taken during the event may be used by the organizers for documentation and promotional purposes and are therefore waived of any compensation.

Will the event push through even if it rains?

Rain or shine we will welcome the All-New Toyota Vios with a bang!
